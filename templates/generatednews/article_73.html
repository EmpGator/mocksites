Faith communities: how have you been affected by coronavirus?

We would like to hear about the pandemic’s effect on your religion’s death rituals and on the way you worship and meet

Whether you have celebrated a barmitzvah online, are accessing prayers and reflections in a different way, or participating in virtual Iftar we would like to hear from you.
